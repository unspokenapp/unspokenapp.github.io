7. Additional Provisions for California Residents

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act ("CCPA"), we are mandated to provide California residents with extra details concerning the collection and use of their personal information.

We categorically state that the categories of personal information we collect from California residents depend largely on the services utilized. These categories can include identifiers like your Unspoken username, email address, and phone number. In addition, we might collect audiovisual information through pictures, audio, or video content submitted to the App or when you participate in a survey. Based on other data collected, we might also make inferences for purposes such as recommending content, advertising, and analytics.

As a resident of California, you are entitled to several additional rights under the CCPA. These rights include, but are not limited to:

  • Right to Opt-Out: You can opt-out of any sale or sharing of your personal information.
  • Right to Know: You have the right to request information regarding the categories of personal data we collect, use, and share, and also specific pieces of personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Delete: You have the right to request deletion of personal data we collected from you.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: You have the right to non-discriminatory treatment for exercising your CCPA rights.
